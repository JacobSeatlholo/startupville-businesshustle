Welcome to Startupville

The Startupville event series is designed to create a safe environment for entrepreneurs, authors, creatives, digital nomads, and more to sit together and have non intrusive conversations about entrepreneurship, strategy and the world of business and open-source resources accessible to/for particular industries online.

Who is this event for?

Startupville is for creatives, digital nomads, entrepreneurs, writers, researchers, traders or employed individuals interested in alternative ways of possibly using their skill and knowledge to make money?. Come and learn how you can leverage knowledge, data and skill through active engagement with like-minded people.

How do I attend?

The event takes place on129 Bree Street in Cape Town. There are limited seats therefore online bookings are essential
